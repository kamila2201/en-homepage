About me

My name is Kamila Maciąg. I was born on 22 January 2002 in Tarnobrzeg. I spent nineteen years of my life in my hometown. After graduating from high school, I decided to go to college. I chose the AGH University Krakow and the field of Management and Production Engineering.

However, after the first year of studies, I knew that this was not the field I wanted to pursue in the future. I started to wonder: "How can I change my career path?". That's when I decided to take a programming course because I had always been attracted to computers and technology. A year and a half has passed since then, and I am only now taking my studies seriously. I followed my heart and decided to take a dean's leave to fully focus on the things that interest me and find my own path in life.

Hobbies

I have loved reading since childhood. It started with youth novels. My favorite genre at the moment is crime fiction. Sometimes I also read books about psychology and personal development.

Since I moved out of my family home, I started cooking. I really like experimenting in the kitchen and making different dishes. I try to eat healthy and nutritious meals, but sometimes I bake a cake.

My next passion is traveling. So far, I have visited many places in Poland, but I have also been to Düsseldorf, Barcelona and Paris. The visit to Germany and France was work-related, so unfortunately I didn't get to see much there, but I plan to go back!
